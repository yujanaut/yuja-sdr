Blackboard Ally Top Competitor
Why we win
  • More alternative formats — audio, ePub, electronic braille, immersive reader, translated versions
  • Deeper LMS integration — works natively inside Canvas, Blackboard, D2L, and Moodle
  • YuJa owns the full ecosystem — no third-party dependency, single vendor relationship
  • Dedicated CSM and 24/7 support at no extra cost — Ally support tiers are add-ons
They say "We already have Blackboard Ally."
You say "Ally is the most common tool we come across — and it does accessibility scoring well. Where Panorama tends to pull ahead is on the breadth of alternative formats and the fact that it's fully embedded in your LMS experience rather than sitting on top of it. Has the range of formats students can access been a conversation at all?"
ReadSpeaker Competitor
Why we win
  • Full accessibility suite vs. read-aloud only — Panorama covers alt formats, remediation, and scoring
  • Native LMS integration — ReadSpeaker often requires separate implementation work
  • One platform, one vendor — ReadSpeaker is a point solution layered on top of existing tools
  • No per-user licensing model — Panorama scales without cost spikes
They say "We use ReadSpeaker for accessibility."
You say "ReadSpeaker does text-to-speech really well — that's its core strength. Panorama covers that and goes further: alternative format generation, accessibility scoring on all course content, and remediation guidance built directly into the LMS. Is read-aloud the primary need, or is the broader accessibility picture something you're trying to solve?"
Brickfield Competitor
Why we win
  • Multi-LMS support — Brickfield is Moodle-only; Panorama works across Canvas, Blackboard, D2L, and Moodle
  • Richer alternative format library — audio, ePub, braille, immersive reader, translations
  • YuJa's scale and support infrastructure vs. a smaller vendor
  • Proven in US higher ed — strong reference base of institutions that have switched from Ally
They say "We use Brickfield for our Moodle accessibility."
You say "Brickfield is a solid Moodle-specific tool. The main limitation we hear is that it's Moodle-only — so if your institution ever moves LMS or runs multiple platforms, you're starting over. Panorama works natively across all the major LMS platforms. Is Moodle your long-term LMS, or is that something that could change?"
